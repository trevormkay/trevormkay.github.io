The Challenge

Finding the signal from the noise

Isentia is a market leader in the media monitoring game. Their platform, Mediaportal, has been providing brands up to date mentions in the news for many years now.

Times have changed though. Clients are now looking not just for mentions, but also intelligence and understand of how their brand is being portrayed in the news. It was time to modernise Mediaportal.

The Solution

One story gives a thousand insights

We wanted to change the way users browse their media coverage. Today they are heavily focused on individual items in the media, but an individual item never lives alone. It’s always part of a story.

Stories is a new way to browse the media. Related items are clustered in to different stories and presented in a more visual fashion. This allows a user to quickly see an overview of their brand in the media.

The Process

Client driven design

Mediaportal is used by a wide range of clients. From big name brands, to government agencies, and even little PR firms. A client base so diverse meant it was important to interview a wide set of clients. Clients were shown various prototypes and their feedback helped to inform the final product.

Sketches of various Mediaportal components

Rejected by interviewed users as they didn’t want their tool looking like a news site

Bringing insights to users meant not just introducing new tools, but also a fresh new look to make sure everything was consistent. I introduced a fresh new look with crisp blues, clean typography, and consistent iconography.

No page was left untouched. Even the no data states were improved to be helpful and provide a splash of colour.
