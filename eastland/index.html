The Challenge

Connecting the physical with the digital

As part of a $900m redevelopment of the Eastland Shopping Centre in Victoria, QIC were looking to create a next generation retail platform that enhances the path to purchase for customers and retailers.

The Solution

Handoff and you're on your way

Beacon enabled kiosks in-centre enable customers with the Eastland Mobile app to get wayfinding directions, shopping guides and other services including pay for parking, concierge and click and collect.

The Process

Hit the ground sprinting

The scope of this project required pulling together a team of designers and developers to work on the many facets. As an Interface Designer I worked collaboratively to come up with novel solutions and oversee implementation. An agile approach was chosen as this allowed the app, website, totem, and CMS to be worked on in tandem. The project consisted of 15 team members, working on 4 interfaces, over 8 sprints.

Sketching out the handoff process

A baseline grid was introduced to keep a visual rhythm to the design as well as keeping consistency with the other platforms.

Eastland was only the beginning. The Eastland app is a template for the other shopping centres in QIC’s portfolio. This required taking each centre’s branding and applying it appropriately to the wireframes.
