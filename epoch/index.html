Epoch

Epoch started out life as a widget for macOS called Percount, developed by Gabe Shabazian. Gabe was looking to take that widget and make it a native iPhone app.

Solution

Percount grows up

Bringing Percount to the iPhone meant taking full advantage of the beautiful screen and hardware. Not only was the interface of Percount scaled up, it became iOS appropriate by using a card based design to swipe through all your countdowns. Location pinning was another feature made available to us based on the hardware of the iPhone. It was a defining feature that other countdown apps did not have.

The original Percount widget

Process

Going native

The brief for Epoch was to use the card based layout of the original iPhone Weather app. Cards worked really well as they allowed for multiple countdowns while making full use of the screen for images.
