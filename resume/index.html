UI/UX Designer for aussieBum

May 2016 - October 2016

My role was to improve the user experience of aussieBum's offerings. This included reworking the UX for key areas of the website taking in to account user feedback on pain points, and creating a new style guide with a modern look. An app was also designed as a new way to connect with customers. I was also involved with monitoring analytics for site performance against KPIs and recommending improvements.

Interactive Designer for MullenLowe Profero

April 2013 - April 2016

During my time at MullenLowe Profero I worked for a varied set of clients including QIC, Johnson & Johnson, Randstad, Western Union, Pizza Hut, and Nutricia. This involved producing a variety of digital and physical work. I was involved in projects at all the different stages a project can be in: UX, art direction, template rollout, front end build, template integration, and maintenance. I also created a lot of images for social channels.

I worked with a wide range of tools including Sketch, Adobe Creative Suite, Sass, Gulp, JIRA, Bamboo, Basecamp, Slack, and many more. Several projects were also run agile.

UI Designer for Gabe Shabazian

Contract: July 2009

I took the OS X widget, Percount, that Gabe Shabazian had made and created a design that was suitable as an iOS app. This involved significantly redesigning the interface to include features that made good use of what iOS has to offer such as MapKit. The app was called Epoch and was available on the App Store during 2010.

Web Designer for Conicom
